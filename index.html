Got Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

When should I take Alpine Reset?

+

Mix 1 scoop with 24 oz of cold water and drink it 45–60 minutes before your first drink. This gives the formula time to prime your liver's detox pathways, restore electrolytes, and activate the protective ingredients before alcohol enters your system. Do not exceed 2 servings per 24 hours.

Why do I feel a tingle after drinking it?

+

The harmless skin tingle is caused by Beta-Alanine — a naturally occurring amino acid. It's a completely normal, benign sensation that confirms Alpine Reset is active in your system. It typically kicks in 10–20 minutes after drinking and fades within 30 minutes.

What makes Alpine Reset different from other products?

+

Most hangover products are just B vitamins and electrolytes in a pretty package. Alpine Reset contains 19 active ingredients at study-level doses — including 1,225mg DHM, 600mg NAC, 1,000mg Prickly Pear, and 700mg Milk Thistle. Every dose is based on published clinical research, not industry convention. It's also manufactured in an FDA-registered, cGMP-certified facility and third-party tested for purity and potency.

Can I take it if I'm not planning to drink?

+

Yes. The electrolyte and vitamin stack in Alpine Reset is excellent for everyday hydration and recovery. Many customers use it post-workout, while traveling, or as a daily wellness supplement. The formula supports liver health, cellular energy, and hydration regardless of alcohol consumption.

Is Alpine Reset safe?

+

Alpine Reset is formulated for adults 21 and over. It is not intended for pregnant or nursing women, or for individuals with kidney or liver disease. If you are taking medications, please consult a physician before use. Manufactured in an FDA-registered, cGMP-certified facility and third-party tested for purity and potency.
