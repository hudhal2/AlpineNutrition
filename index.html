Got Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How does Alpine Reset work?

+

Alpine Reset combines a high-dose electrolyte blend, cellular hydration technology, and B-vitamins to counteract the three main causes of a rough morning after drinking: dehydration, nutrient depletion, and inflammation. Mix one scoop with 16 oz of water, drink before bed, and wake up feeling ready.

When should I take Alpine Reset?

+

For best results, take one scoop with 16 oz of water before going to sleep. This allows the electrolyte and vitamin blend to work while your body is in recovery mode during sleep. You can also take a second serving in the morning if needed.

Is Alpine Reset all-natural?

+

Yes. Alpine Reset uses natural fruit flavors, plant-based colors, and no artificial sweeteners, dyes, or preservatives. We use stevia leaf extract as a natural sweetener. Every ingredient is clean-label and non-GMO.

How many servings are in one container?

+

Each container of Alpine Reset contains 15 servings (10.58 oz / 300g). Each serving is one level scoop mixed with 12–16 oz of cold water.

Can I take Alpine Reset if I'm not drinking?

+

Absolutely. Alpine Reset is an excellent everyday hydration and recovery supplement. Athletes use it post-workout, travelers use it for jet lag, and many people use it daily as a superior alternative to standard sports drinks.
